Lambda Expression to compute Total SurfaceArea of Cylinder.

`
import math
surfaceArea = lambda radius, height: 2 * (math.pi) * radius * height * (radius + height)
`{{execute}}

`
totalSurfaceArea = surfaceArea(3,4)
`{{execute}}

`
print("Total Surface Area of Cylinder is : ", totalSurfaceArea)
`{{execute}}
